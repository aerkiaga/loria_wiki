====== Lead (II) oxide (PbO) ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}
**Name:** ''loria:lead_oxide''
**Description:** Lead (II) oxide (PbO)
**Crumbly:** 2

**Lead (II) oxide (PbO)** is an intermediate crafting material that is produced from {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead_sulfate.png}} [[Lead (II) sulfate (PbSO4)]]  and necessary to craft  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead.png}} [[Lead (Pb)]].

====== Obtaining ======

===== Refiner crafting =====
1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead_sulfate.png}} [[Lead (II) sulfate (PbSO4)]] + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/bucket.png}} [[Empty bucket]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead_oxide.png}} **Lead (II) oxide (PbO)** + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/bucket_sulfur_trioxide.png}} [[Sulfur trioxide bucket]]
This recipe takes 4 units of time.

====== Usage ======

===== Refiner crafting =====
1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead_oxide.png}} **Lead (II) oxide (PbO)** + 1x  {{./pasted_image001.png}} [[Hydrogen balloon]] + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/bucket.png}} [[Empty bucket]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead.png}} [[Lead (Pb)]] + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_empty_balloon.png}} [[Empty balloon]] + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/bucket_water.png}} [[Water bucket]]
This recipe takes 2 units of time.
