====== Empty balloon ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}

The **empty balloon** is a versatile crafting ingredient, serving as a container to temporarily store and transport various gases across recipes, or release them into the ambient.

====== Obtaining ======

===== Initial inventory =====
Every player will first spawn with one empty balloon in their inventory, along with two  {{./pasted_image003010.png}} [[Oxygen balloon]].

===== Placing =====
Left-clicking anywhere while holding any filled balloon will empty it, releasing the contained gas as its corresponding node, and yielding an empty balloon.
