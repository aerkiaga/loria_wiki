====== Lead (II) selenide (PbSe) ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}
**Name:** ''loria:lead_selenide''
**Description:** Lead (II) selenide (PbSe)
**Cracky:** 1

**Lead (II) selenide (PbSe)** is an intermediate crafting material that is necessary to craft  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/furnace/textures/furnace_gas_front.png}} [[Furnace|Gas furnace (PbSe)]].

====== Obtaining ======

===== Refiner crafting =====
1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead.png}} [[Lead (Pb)]] + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_selenium.png}} [[Selenium (Se)]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead_selenide.png}} **Lead (II) selenide (PbSe)**
This recipe takes 5 units of time.

====== Usage ======

===== Inventory crafting =====
3x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_lead_selenide.png}} **Lead (II) selenide (PbSe)** → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/furnace/textures/furnace_gas_front.png}} [[Furnace|Gas furnace (PbSe)]]
