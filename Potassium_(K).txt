====== Potassium (K) ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}
**Name:** ''loria:potassium''
**Description:** Potassium (K)
**Cracky:** 1
**Fuel:** 2

**Potassium** is a solid node that is only useful as compact storage for  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium_ingot.png}} [[Potassium ingot]].

====== Obtaining ======

===== Refiner crafting =====
9x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium_ingot.png}} [[Potassium ingot]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium.png}} **Potassium (K)**
This recipe takes 4 units of time.

====== Usage ======

===== Fuel =====
Potassium burns for 2 units of time, much less than  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium_ingot.png}} [[Potassium ingot]], even though it takes 9 of the latter to craft it.

===== Refiner crafting =====
1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium.png}} **Potassium (K) **→ 9x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium_ingot.png}} [[Potassium ingot]]
This recipe takes 4 units of time.
