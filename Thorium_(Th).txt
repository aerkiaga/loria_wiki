====== Thorium (Th) ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}
**Name:** ''loria:thorium''

**Cracky:** 1

**Thorium (Th)** is a solid [[Radiation|radioactive]] element that mainly acts as an intermediate product in the crafting of  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/furnace/textures/furnace_thorium_front.png}} [[Furnace|Gas furnace (ThO2)]].

====== Obtaining ======

===== Refiner crafting =====
1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_thorium_iodide.png}} [[Thorium iodide (ThI4)]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_thorium.png}} **Thorium (Th)** + 2x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_iodine.png}} [[Iodine (I)]]
This recipe takes 3 units of time.

===== Furnace crafting =====
9x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_thorium_ingot.png}} [[Thorium ingot]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_thorium.png}} **Thorium (Th)**
This recipe takes 4 units of time and requires a  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/furnace/textures/furnace_thorium_front.png}} [[Furnace|Gas furnace (ThO2)]].
