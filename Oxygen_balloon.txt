====== Oxygen balloon ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}

====== Obtaining ======

===== Initial inventory =====
Every player will first spawn with one oxygen balloon in their inventory, as well as a second balloon in their "Oxygen" slot.

====== Usage ======

===== Breathing =====
The player will gradually consume the  {{./pasted_image003.png}} **Oxygen balloon** in their "Oxygen" slot until it becomes an  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_empty_balloon.png}} [[Empty balloon]], and will start suffering damage if there is no oxygen left in it or the item is removed.

===== Fuel oxidizer =====
The [[Refiner]] and both varieties of [[Furnace]] require one  {{./pasted_image003.png}} **Oxygen balloon** in their "Gas" slot to turn on, and will gradually apply wear to it until it becomes an  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_empty_balloon.png}} [[Empty balloon]].

===== Placing =====
Left-clicking anywhere while holding this item will empty the bottle and place an [[Oxygen]] node that will propagate itself to fill a volume proportional to the bottle's content. While oxygen as a gas is entirely invisible, it will oxidize  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_cinnabar.png}} [[Cinnabar (HgS)]] to solid  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_mercury.png}} [[Mercury (Hg)]] and burst into flames upon contact with an active  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/furnace/textures/furnace_gas_front.png}} [[Furnace|Gas furnace (PbSe)]].
