====== Empty bucket ======

{{./pasted_image.png}}
The **empty bucket** is a pivotal crafting ingredient and tool, allowing to contain and transport liquids, as well as picking them up or placing them in the world.

====== Obtaining ======

===== Initial inventory =====
Every player will first spawn with two empty buckets in their inventory.

===== Placing =====
Left-clicking anywhere while holding any filled bucket will empty it, releasing the contained liquid as its corresponding node, and yielding an empty bucket.

====== Usage ======

===== Picking up =====
Left-clicking on any liquid source node while holding an empty bucket will pick up the liquid, producing the corresponding bucket variant.
