====== Cinnabar ======

{{./pasted_image001.png}}
**Name:** ''loria:cinnabar''
**Description:** Cinnabar (HgS)
**Cracky:** 2
**Fuel:** 1

**Cinnabar (HgS)** is a widely distributed stone-like mineral. Often, it fulfills a "default" ground type role. Cinnabar is rich in ores, and the material itself is combustible, making it a very useful finding.
Cinnabar and all ores based on it evaporate and disappear on contact with [[Fire|fire]]. Upon coming in contact with [[Oxigen|oxigen]], cinnabar turns into  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_mercury.png}} [[Mercury (Hg)]] and releases [[Sulfur dioxide|sulfur dioxide]]. 

====== Obtaining ======

===== Mining =====
Due to acting as a "default" node, cinnabar will appear in patches on any exposed stone surface, replacing the usual material for the [[Biomes|biome]]. This occurs either as a mapgen glitch (producing large, flat blobs) or the non-glitchy replacement of dungeon nodes (producing "rooms" made of cinnabar).

Additionally, cinnabar is the stone node in the [[Biomes#redland|Redland]] biome, making it trivial to obtain large quantities of it if in that biome.

====== Usage ======

===== Fuel =====
Cinnabar is a weak but easily obtainable fuel, burning for 1 unit of time. The cinnabar-based version of  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_potassium_ore.png}} [[Potassium ore]], much like other ores of potassium, is a fuel too, burning for 3 units.

===== Refiner crafting =====
1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_cinnabar.png}} **Cinnabar (HgS)** + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/bucket.png}} [[Empty bucket]] → 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/loria_sulfur.png}} [[Sulfur (S)]] + 1x  {{~/.minetest/games/loria/mods/loria/textures/bucket_mercury.png}} [[Mercury bucket]]
This recipe takes 5 units of time.
